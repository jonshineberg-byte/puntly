Puntly

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated April 2026

Puntly is a social betting club app that lets friends share bets, track performance, and compete on leaderboards.

What We Collect

  • Account information — email address and display name when you sign up
  • Profile data — nickname you choose for each club
  • Bet data — bet details you enter or photograph, including stake, odds, selection, and outcome
  • Photos — bet slip images you choose to upload from your camera or photo library
  • Device token — a push notification token to send you bet updates (only with your permission)
  • Timezone offset — used solely to send notifications at an appropriate local time

How We Use It

We use your data only to operate the app — showing your bets to club members, calculating leaderboards, and sending notifications you've opted into.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except as necessary to provide the service.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase, hosted in the United States. Bet slip images are stored in Firebase Storage. See Google Firebase's privacy policy for more detail.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time from within the app
  • Access a copy of your personal data on request
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via iPhone Settings → Notifications

Contact
